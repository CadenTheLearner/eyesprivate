Dr. Matt

Dr. Matthew DeGroot grew up in a very small town in southeastern South Dakota. He graduated from the University of South Dakota with a bachelor’s in Biology and minor in Chemistry. Dr. DeGroot received a Doctor of Optometry from the Illinois College of Optometry.

After graduation, Dr. DeGroot practiced at Lossman Eye Care in Park Ridge for three years serving as an adjunct clinical preceptor in primary care and contact lenses for the Illinois College of Optometry and Pennsylvania College of Optometry. In 2006 he then moved on and spent three years at a laser vision correction center seeing approximately 30,000 pre and post operative LASIK and PRK patients. Dr. DeGroot started Envision Eye Care within a Chicago Costco in November of 2008.

In January 2023 he happily returned to Park Ridge teaming up with his wife Dr. Melissa DeGroot to concentrate on their private practice patients.

Dr. DeGroot has enjoyed volunteering for the American Red Cross as a first aid and CPR instructor. He has also had the privilege to be an attending optometrist at a rehabilitation and nursing facility located in Des Plaines.

Dr. Matthew DeGroot loves spending time with his family doing such activities as boating, hiking, biking, camping, fishing, and swimming. He is also an avid fan of NFL football.

Dr. Melissa

Dr. Melissa DeGroot (Grandinetti) grew up in Lincolnwood, IL attending St. Mary of the Woods and Niles West High School. She graduated from Loyola University with a bachelor’s in Biology. Dr. DeGroot received a Doctor of Optometry from the Illinois College of Optometry.

Dr. DeGroot then started her own practice where she could concentrate on taking the time to be with her patients and understand all of their eye care needs. She works with her patients to determine the best glasses and contacts for them from easing computer strain to seeing the green street signs in Park Ridge! Her patients are treated like family and she has seen many of them for almost 20 years.

In January 2023 she welcomed her husband Dr. Matthew DeGroot to her practice. They have teamed up providing complementary vision care.

Dr. Melissa DeGroot loves spending time with her husband and children. She enjoys many activities with them including hiking, biking, tennis, and soccer.
