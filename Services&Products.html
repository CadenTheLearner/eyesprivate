Eye Exams:

At our eye exams, we offer a comprehensive evaluation of your visual health, testing for a range of conditions including nearsightedness, farsightedness, astigmatism, and presbyopia, as well as screening for serious eye diseases such as glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. Our state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment ensures precise measurements and accurate assessments, while our experienced optometrists provide thorough examinations to not only correct your vision but also safeguard your eye health. With a focus on both preventive care and effective treatment, our eye exams are designed to help you achieve optimal vision and maintain long-term ocular health.

Contact Lenses:

Our contact lens services include a detailed evaluation to find the best lenses for your vision and lifestyle. Myopia (Nearsightedness) Management:

Our myopia management program is designed to address the increasing prevalence of nearsightedness and provide effective solutions to slow its progression in children. Through a combination of advanced treatments such as specialized contact lenses and atropine eye drops, we tailor a management plan to each individual’s needs. These methods work to stabilize or reduce the rate of myopia progression, helping to prevent the development of more severe vision problems in the future. Our comprehensive approach includes thorough eye exams to accurately assess myopia and ongoing monitoring to ensure the effectiveness of the chosen treatment.

In addition to these treatments, our myopia management program focuses on educating families about myopia, its risks, and preventive measures. We provide guidance on lifestyle adjustments, such as increasing outdoor activities and reducing screen time, which can contribute to better eye health. Our goal is to offer a proactive and evidence-based approach to myopia management, helping patients maintain healthy vision and a high quality of life.

Our methods Slow Progression by over 50%.
